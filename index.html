Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of LCGC Resolute Group of Companies. This policy delineates the methods by which we collect, utilize, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in adherence to the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and all other pertinent laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from employees:

  • Personal Information: That we collect user’s personal information including but not limited to that of full name, date of birth (DOB), date of joining (DOJ), contact number, employee ID, email ID and photo.
  • Content and Activity: We collect information about your posts, including likes and comments. Additionally, we collect information about your event participation, poll responses, and any other interactions on this website.

2. How We Use Information

  • Facilitate Communication and Interaction Among Employees: The primary purpose of collection of information is to enable effective communication and collaboration among employees within the group of companies. This includes facilitating posts, comments, shares, and interactions on the website.
  • Engage in User Communication and Address User Inquiries: We use user information to communicate with you, including sending important updates, notifications, and responding to your inquiries or requests for support.
  • To Enhance the Website Features: We analyze the information we collect to understand how employees use this website, identify trends, and improve our features and services.
  • Promote Safety and Security On and Off our Services: We take measures to ensure the safety and security of our employees' interactions within the website. This includes monitoring for fraudulent or unauthorized activities, enforcing our policies, and protecting against security threats.

3. How We Share Information

  • Employees: Your profile information (full name, photo, and about us) will be visible to other employees. Your posts, comments, and interactions will also be visible as per the website’s settings.
  • Group Companies: Your information may be shared among the companies within the group to facilitate inter-company communication and collaboration.
  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers who support our business operations, such as hosting services and IT support who are bound by a confidentiality of the information.
  • Law Enforcement or Legal Requests: We may disclose information with law enforcement or in response to legal requests in the situations outlined in our Data Policy.

4. How You Can Manage and Delete Information

  • Manage Your Information: User will have the right to access and edit the information provided in user profile at any time.
  • Delete Your Account: You can request to delete your account by contacting the HR Department. When you leave the organization under any circumstances, your account shall be deleted by the web admin which shall include but is not limited to your posts, photos, comments and other updates etc.

5. Data Security

We are committed to safeguarding the security of your personal information. We implement a range of technical, administrative, and physical security measures designed to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. Our security practices are in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is exclusively for the employees of Resolute Group of Companies. That the user with eligibility criteria of less than 13 years only will enjoy access towards the website and are subjected to disclose the relevant information for the same. That we will not record any information who is not age eligibility criteria to access the website. If there is any user discovered under the age of 13 years makes an entry to the website, we will take necessary measures to erase all information pertinent to that user.

7. Changes to This Policy

That the admin and relevant host parties will reserve the right to make timely changes to this policy and immediately issue an alert to all the users on any modification and amendments. That the user deserves the right to agree or disagree on the alert after which appropriate guidance and notification will be communicated to the users.
